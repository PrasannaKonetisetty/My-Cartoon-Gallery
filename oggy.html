Oggy and the Cockroaches

About Oggy&Cockroages

Oggy is a lazy cat 🐱 who loves watching TV 📺 and eating all day 🍕.
But three cockroaches – Dee Dee, Joey, and Marky 🪳🪳🪳 – always ruin his peace 😡.
Their fights are epic and full of madness 💥.
Oggy sometimes wins, but mostly ends up in trouble 😂.
Even though they fight, there are moments where they save each other 😮.
Oggy’s cousin Jack also gets involved and adds more fun 🤯.
There are times Oggy dreams of love ❤️ and peace but it always ends in chaos.
The slapstick humor is legendary and never gets old 😆.
Some episodes show Oggy's loneliness too, and we feel sad 😞.
It’s a cartoon that shows life is unpredictable but always entertaining 🎢✨.
