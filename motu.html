About MOTU-PATHULU

Motu loves samosas 🍽️ and Patlu is the brainy one 🧠.
They live in Furfuri Nagar and face daily adventures 🤹.
Dr. Jhatka gives crazy gadgets and Ghasitaram says “20 years ka experience” 🤖.
Inspector Chingam tries to catch thieves but ends up falling 😂.
Motu gets power after eating samosas and fights bad guys 🥷.
Their friendship is very deep – always helping each other 💪.
Some episodes show their sadness when someone leaves town 😢.
They even risk their lives to protect others 🛡️.
Even villains like John the Don become funny with their actions 😆.
It’s a cartoon full of friendship, courage, and endless fun 🎊.
